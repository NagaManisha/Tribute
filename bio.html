About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the most successful and talented cricketers of his generation, known for his aggressive batting style and leadership.

Career Highlights

  • Made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2008.
  • Captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats.
  • Led India to numerous Test and ODI victories.

Records

Virat Kohli holds several records, including:

  • Fastest century in ODIs.
  • Most runs in a calendar year in ODIs.
  • Multiple centuries in Test matches and ODIs.
